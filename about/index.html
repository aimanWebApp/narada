About Us

Zhejiang Narada Power Source Co., Ltd. (Stock Code: 300068) provides products, system integration and services with lithium-ion batteries and lead batteries as the core for the four application fields of data center, smart energy storage, industrial backup and green travel. At the same time, Narada has created two industrial closed loops of "lead battery cycle industrial chain" and "lithium battery cycle industrial chain". The company has carried out sales in 158 countries and regions around the world, and the company's brand "Narada" is famous all over the world.

Technical Innovation
Narada is a national high-tech enterprise with such innovative platforms as national enterprise technology center, CNAS accredited laboratory, postdoctoral workstation, academician workstation, Zhejiang Equipment and Electronics Research Institute and so on. Narada has core technology patents in new materials, new technologies and new structures of battery power supply, has led and participated in the formulation of a number of international, national and industrial standards, and has become one of the top-100 enterprises in China's light industry and electronic information industry for many consecutive years.

History
1994

In September, Hangzhou Narada Power Source Co., Ltd. was established

1996

In March, the first batch of lead storage and seal battery production line was put into operation

1997

In September, Zhejiang Narada Power Source Industry Co., Ltd. was established

2000

In September, Zhejiang Narada Power Source Industry Co., Ltd. was changed to be Zhejiang Narada Power Source Co., Ltd.

2001

In April, the company invested and established Sunrise Power Co., Ltd. (National Engineering Research Center for Fuel Cell and Hydrogen Source Technology)

In October, Shanghai Narada Ruibao Energy Technology Co., Ltd. was registered and established in Shanghai to carry out business in the lithium-ion battery industry

2005

In November, the first overseas branch, “Narada Asia Pacific Pte.Ltd,” was registered and established in Singapore

2006

In March, Narada Research Institute was established

In July, Hangzhou Narada Energy Technology Co., Ltd. was established

2010

In April, Narada was listed on China GEM successfully with stock code of 300068

In November, Narada provided energy storage batteries exclusively for the first large-scale practical application energy storage project in China, Zhejiang dongfushan new energy project, and carried out business in the new energy storage industry

2011

In September, Narada acquired Jieshou Narada Huayu Power Source Co., Ltd., and carried out business in the power battery industry

2012

In January, Narada increased capital in Chengdu Guojian New Energy Co., Ltd

In April, construction of Hangzhou Narada Power Technology Co., Ltd. (lithium ion battery production base) commenced.

2013

In November, the company’s technology center was successfully appraised as a nationally recognized enterprise technology center

In November, Zhejiang Institute of Equipment Electronic Information was established

2014

In January, Wuhan Narada New Energy Technology Co., Ltd. was registered and established

2015

In June, Narada acquired Anhui Huabo Renewable Resources Technology Co., Ltd. to start to create a closed-loop of the lead battery industry

In December, the company's private offering of shares was approved by CSRC

2016

In December, Zhejiang Narada Energy Network Co., Ltd. was established to occupy the highest position in the field of global energy storage

The company has successively held shares of KH Automotive Technologies Co., Ltd. (KHAT) and Zhixing Hongyuan Automobile Co., Ltd. to accelerate the layout of new energy vehicle industrial chain

2017

In July, the assets reorganization of Anhui Huabo Renewable Resources Technology Co., Ltd. (a subsidiary) was completed

2018

In February, Anhui Huabo Renewable Resources Technology Co., Ltd., Jieshou Narada Huayu Power Source Co., Ltd. and Lin'an Narada (three subsidiaries of Narada) were named as the national green factories.

In November, the demonstration project of high-efficiency and green treatment and comprehensive recycling and reuse of waste lead battery was put into operation smoothly, a closed-loop of the lead battery industry was formed.

2019

In December, Anhui Huabo Renewable Resources Technology Co., Ltd. won the first prize of Science and Technology Award in Anhui Province.

2020

In June, Anhui Narada Huabo New Materials Technology Co., Ltd. was established, Narada started to carry out business in the field of lithium battery recycling

In June, Anhui Narada Huatuo New Energy Technology Co., Ltd. was established

In November, the national science and technology major project "the independent development of CAP1400 super-capacity and safe-class valve regulated storage battery" successfully passed the acceptance, and this achievement successfully filled in the international gap. The project was led by Narada and jointly undertaken by Shanghai Nuclear Engineering Research and Design Institute Co., Ltd.

In November, Narada was selected into two green manufacturing lists at the same time, namely "national green supply chain management demonstration enterprise" and "green design products"
